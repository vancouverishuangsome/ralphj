ralph peragine himself could not be reached. His last known public appearance was at the Coquitlam Sunwood grand opening in September 2022. Since then, the man who once posed proudly beside the fresh-pressed juice bar and the 1,500-variety B.C. wine wall has seemingly perfected the ultimate disappearing act — one that leaves no forwarding address, no final text, just empty shelves where a relationship used to be.

Meanwhile, the stores he helped open continue to thrive. Shoppers in Coquitlam still line up for grilled steaks and Napoli-style pizza. In Penticton, the Mediterranean bar does steady business. And somewhere, perhaps in the next city on the list, another woman is probably hearing the same smooth lines about “building something real this time.”

Because for Ralph ralph peragine, every new Save-On-Foods is just another fresh start — for everyone except the women he leaves behind.
